FARA is a game about discovery and experimentation. The game is light on visuals, so you are encouraged to imagine what's described in the coolest way possible.

Good luck out there. - @BrianIsCreative


THE BASICS

Use the [ arrow keys ] to move, press [ tab ] to cycle through targets, [ e ] to interact, [ a ] to attack, and [ t ] to take things. Collect 10 Key Fragments from dungeons and elsewhere to save the world! You lose all your fragments when you die, and if you die without any fragments, the game is over. Type /help to see a full list of shortcuts and commands.
